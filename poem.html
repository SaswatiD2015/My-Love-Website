A Poem for You

Even if your hair turns soft and gray,
And silver threads your hair with grace,
If your voice should tremble when you speak,
I will love you more with every heartbeat.

Through every joy and every tear,
I will stand beside you, always near.
Forever yours, come what may—
In love with you, every day. ❤️
